Training program

obedience training "Mastering basic commands is the first step in dog training. Our basic obedience training includes sitting, waiting, shaking hands, getting down and more to make your dog more manageable and improve the quality of family life."

Behavior Modification "We provide professional behavior modification services for dogs' bad behaviors, such as biting, barking, and urinating in public. Help your dog develop good behavior through patient guidance and positive motivation."

Skills Training "Want your dog to learn more advanced skills? Our advanced skills include obstacle jumping, tracking, searching and more to make your pet an all-around player."

Training method

"We use positive motivation training methods, which allow dogs to learn in a pleasant atmosphere through rewards and encouragement. This method can effectively increase the dog's interest in learning and enhance the training effect."

Gamified training "integrates training into play, allowing dogs to learn skills as they play. Our gamification training exercises the dog's intelligence and enhances the dog's bond with its owner."
